11/11/22 Sound Ceremony
Patrick Millard  7:45 pm - 9:15 pm $40 / $45 day of View More

Sound has been used as a tool for revelation and transformation by many psycho-spiritual traditions throughout history. Vibration itself is fundamental to the structure of our universe, and we are but one result of its ever-unfolding process.

Join us for an evening devoted to exploring this primal connection to sound. You’ll be invited to find a home in your body through guided relaxation and light breath work, setting the stage for a deep and receptive listening experience. It is from here that we can tap the wellspring of our healing.

Biography
Patrick has been engaged with the practice of sound as a tool for meditation since 2016. He holds over 100 hours of training from the WOOM Center and over four years of experience in facilitating sound and breath-work ceremonies alike. Along with over six years of teaching yoga, His passion for music, movement, and people is reflected in the welcoming environments he helps create.

3/25/23 Journey to India
Bryn Chrisman    Click + to expand info View More

I hope you find this note in the sweet space between suns-setting and full moons rising, wistfully longing for adventure and ready for a romance with the faraway land called India.

You are invited…

India Retreat 2023 w/ Bryn through Royal Rajasthan
Jaipur, Udaipur, Jodphur, and finishing our tour at RAAS CHHATRASAGAR luxury lakeside tents in the countryside.

Monday, March 13th – Saturday, March 25th 2023
(We recommend arriving in Delhi on March 12th anytime. We leave as a group on a Domestic India flight from Delhi to Jaipur March 13th)

Rajasthan, set against a sprawling desert landscape, has a regal, grand, and culturally rich history that makes travel here truly epic.

Nothing is better than sunrise yoga/coffee/chai, watching the sky shift from moonlight to sunlight, the astounding colors of subtle Jaipur pink hues to bright Jodphur blues, local sounds of cowbells and prayer, the distinct smell of morning fires cooking up milky chai, spreading the scent of ginger/cardamom into the dawn. I feel nostalgia for these mornings with you even before they happen, along with all the other experiences only India can deliver.

Delight your taste buds, nourish your ears, put a sparkle in your eyes, and – if you love shopping like me – adorn your body with Indian textiles, bangles, gems, and perhaps even gold! We will visit temples during puja (daily rituals of devotion), smell and shop the fabulous spice markets, and experience the modern-day joys of India as well – but we can keep a little mystery here : )

Every day I will guide yoga classes and offer storytelling in carefully selected spots – a palace rooftop, a lakeside pavilion, a desert dune, or a park ripe with bird life.
