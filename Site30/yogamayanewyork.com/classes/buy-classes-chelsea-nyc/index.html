Mat rental is included with all classes - students must clean mats after use with our provided cleaner! Private Mat Storage is available for $20/month or $10/month for Monthly Pass holders. We have a 6-hr cancellation window. Cancellations within 6hrs of a class start time will be considered "late canceled" and the class will be used. Thank you❣

Rules & Requests

Time: We have a 5-minute late policy. Arrive early to give yourself time to sign in and change. Don’t stress, but if you are running late, the class may already be full or the door locked. If you are able to enter late, please enter the room quietly without disrupting others. If you need to leave early, tell the teacher before class and follow their guidelines. Please inform the teacher of any emergency situations and come up with a solution together.

Space: Practice awareness when placing your mat down and make space for yourself and others. It helps to stagger the mats so the front of the mats are not lined up. You may need to adjust your mat as more people enter the room to keep a good spacing vibe.

Shoes: Leave them by the studio entrance or put them inside your bag to bring into the practice room. No shoes in the practice room unless bagged!

Stuff: Please store your valuables on the shelves in the yoga room and not by your mat or in the coatroom. No tech use allowed in practice rooms before, during or after class (a.k.a. ever). Please put your phone on silent (not vibrate) and in your bag. We will provide a small charging station by the shelving units and that is the only place you should charge your phone during class.

Thank you for helping us preserve the sacred space of the practice rooms and overall yoga studio vibe.
